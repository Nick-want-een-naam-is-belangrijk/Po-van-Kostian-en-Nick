Random


This is a small subpage to explain how the random spinoff works.


Every tile has a random type assigned to it. Not every type is as likely, otherwise it just wouldn't even be possible 99% of the time. The chances are:


Air   Solid   Hazard   Water
12   2   1   1

You can choose where you want to start on the y-axis, but you have to start at the left side of the screen. Your objective is to get to the other side of the screen. Good luck!


(Also you can restart with OK. Might come in handy when the level is physically impossible)
